About

Cross Country Evangelism is a ministry founded by Mike Stockwell, a former U.S. Marine who left his landscaping business to serve the Lord full-time. The ministry is dedicated to proclaiming the gospel of Jesus Christ across America, Europe, and Jamaica through open-air preaching, one-on-one evangelism, and gospel tract distribution.

The Plain Gospel

The Plain Gospel by William F. Bell presents the core message of salvation through Jesus Christ. It answers common questions about faith and shares scripture to guide readers toward salvation.
